## 🧾 Eligibility

Check if you qualify for citizenship:

- Minimum residency period
- Legal permanent residency status

## 🌍 Citizenship Application

Basic application details:

### Application Form

**Full Name**  
Your legal full name.

**Current Nationality**  
Your current citizenship.

**Country Applying To**  
The country where you are applying for citizenship.

**Residency Duration**  
How long you have lived in the country.

**Language Proficiency**  
Level of official language ability.
